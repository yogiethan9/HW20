Dieta App

An automated meal planning and delivery service for people on the road.

Research

When conducting user interviews regarding meal prepping while traveling, I found that users found it difficult to eat healthy and avoid fast food while traveling. This led me to the idea of Dieta, which would allow users to plan a trip and create presets within their profile that would allow the app to plan out meals and have them delivered to them automatically.

Problem Statement

Travelers find it difficult to eat healthy while on the road. It can be a struggle to properly plan nutritious meals and avoid the temptation of eating fast food.
